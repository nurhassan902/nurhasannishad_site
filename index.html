MY DESIGN JOURNEY

I didn’t start as a designer — I started as someone who was curious. I often asked questions like, “Why is this interface confusing?” or “How can this experience be made simpler?

That curiosity slowly turned into passion. I began exploring colors, layouts, and user flows, but soon realized that design is not just about visuals — it’s about solving problems, improving usability, and creating meaningful experiences for people.

Over time, I’ve worked on websites, dashboards, and digital products. Each project taught me something new — from understanding user behavior to refining details through feedback and continuous iteration.

Today, I design with a clear purpose — to create simple, intuitive, and human-centered digital experiences that connect user needs with business goals.
